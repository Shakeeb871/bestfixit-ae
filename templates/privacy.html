Privacy Policy

Home · Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Best Fix Technical Services ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects the information you share with us when you use our website or request our services.

Information we collect

When you contact us or book a service, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, service address and details of the job you need done. We also collect basic technical data such as your IP address and browser type when you visit our website.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide quotes.
  • To schedule, deliver and follow up on the services you request.
  • To improve our website, services and customer experience.
  • To send service updates or offers where you have agreed to receive them.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with our technicians and trusted partners to the extent needed to carry out your requested service, or where required by law.

Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against loss, misuse and unauthorised access.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at info@bestfixit.ae.

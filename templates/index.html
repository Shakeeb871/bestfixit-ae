Do you offer same-day service?
Yes. Book before noon and we can usually have a technician with you the same afternoon, anywhere in Dubai. Emergencies are handled around the clock.
How does your pricing work?
We inspect first, then give you a fixed price before any work begins. No hourly surprises — the quote you approve is what you pay.
Are your technicians licensed?
Every technician is licensed for their trade, background-checked, and — for electrical work — DEWA-approved. We're fully insured on every job.
What warranty do you provide?
All workmanship carries a 12-month guarantee in writing. If anything we fixed fails within that window, we return and put it right at no charge.
Which areas do you cover?
We cover all of Dubai from our base in JVC — villas, apartments and commercial units alike. Ask us about surrounding emirates.
Can I book a recurring maintenance plan?
Absolutely. We offer annual maintenance contracts covering AC servicing, plumbing checks and general upkeep — priced per property.

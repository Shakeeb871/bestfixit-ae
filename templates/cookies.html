This Cookies Policy explains how Best Fix Technical Services uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work properly, remember your preferences and understand how the site is used.

How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies — needed for the website to function correctly.
  • Performance cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.
  • Preference cookies — remember choices you make for a better experience.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect how the website works.
